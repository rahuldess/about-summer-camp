policies & registration —

What you need to know — no surprises.

We believe in being upfront. Here's everything about registration, fees, and how the program works.

✕
no makeups —

No makeup classes

If your child misses a session for any reason — illness, travel, or otherwise — we're unable to offer makeups. Each week is designed as a cohesive experience for the whole group.

$
deposit —

$100 non-refundable deposit

A $100 deposit is required at enrollment to secure your child's spot. It's applied toward your total fee, not added on top, but it is non-refundable under any circumstances.

✓
new students —

$50 registration fee

First-time students pay a one-time $50 registration fee that covers your child's program materials for the summer.

★
early bird —

Register by May 15 — fee waived

New students who complete enrollment by May 15, 2026 have the $50 registration fee waived entirely. Early registration also guarantees your preferred plan and weeks.
